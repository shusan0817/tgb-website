About Us

Professional Outdoor Furniture & Decor Manufacturer Since 1998

Company Profile

Leading manufacturer of outdoor leisure products in China

He Xing Lighting (TGB) was founded in 1998 in Guangdong, China. With 15+ years of development, we have grown into a comprehensive outdoor leisure products manufacturer with 9 global factories, 3000+ employees, and $80 million annual revenue.

Our product lines include outdoor decor (PE rattan, PE wood, Textilene), inflatable products (commercial pools, water parks, toys), and outdoor furniture (lounge chairs, dining sets, sofas). We serve clients in North America, Europe, Australia, Japan, and Southeast Asia.
