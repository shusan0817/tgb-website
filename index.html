Furniture Craftsmanship

Why Our Furniture Stands Apart

Every piece we manufacture carries the hallmark of precision engineering, premium materials, and relentless quality standards built over 15+ years.

🌲

FSC-Certified Teak & Premium Wood

We source only Grade-A FSC-certified teak from sustainably managed forests. Our teak contains natural oils that repel water, resist insects, and prevent warping — no chemical treatment required.

NATURAL OIL CONTENT ≥ 8%
🔩

Marine-Grade Aluminum Frames

All aluminum components use 6061-T6 aerospace alloy with powder-coat finish tested to 1000+ hours salt-spray resistance. Lightweight yet stronger than steel at the same weight.

1000H SALT-SPRAY TESTED
🧵

Solution-Dyed Acrylic Fabric

Cushion fabrics are solution-dyed — color is embedded into the fiber, not just the surface. UV-resistant for 3000+ hours, fade-proof, mold-resistant, and machine washable.

UV RATING 50+
⚙️

Precision Mortise & Tenon Joinery

Traditional woodworking meets modern CNC precision. Each joint is glued with marine-grade adhesive and bolted with stainless steel hardware, rated for 300kg+ static load capacity.

300KG LOAD RATED
🏭

Vertical Integration & In-House Testing

From raw material to finished product — all under one roof. Our in-house lab runs vibration, drop, impact, chemical resistance, and dimensional stability tests before every shipment.

ISO 9001 CERTIFIED
🌦️

All-Climate Engineering

Designed for extremes: -20°C Nordic winters to 50°C desert summers. Zero rust, zero warp, zero fade guarantee backed by our 10-year structural warranty on all premium lines.

10-YEAR WARRANTY
